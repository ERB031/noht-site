Our Story

NOHT is more than a production company — it's a collective of creatives, storytellers, and community builders shaping Atlanta's next chapter.

Where It All Began

NOHT was born out of a shared belief that great stories have the power to connect people, spark change, and leave a lasting mark. What started as late-night conversations between four friends in Atlanta grew into something far bigger than any of us imagined — a creative home where film, art, and purpose come together under one roof.

Our name stands for something personal to each of us, but at its core, NOHT represents a promise: to create with honesty, to produce with intention, and to always give back to the community that raised us. We are filmmakers, yes — but we are also neighbors, collaborators, and advocates for the voices that too often go unheard.

From our very first project, we knew that the work had to mean something beyond the screen. That's why every production we take on carries a thread of purpose — whether it's a narrative short film, a brand campaign, or a documentary made in partnership with a local nonprofit. We believe creativity is at its best when it serves something larger than itself.

Rooted in Atlanta

Atlanta isn't just where we work — it's where we belong. This city's creative energy, cultural richness, and resilient spirit run through everything we do. From the neighborhoods that shaped us to the local artists and organizations we collaborate with, Atlanta is the heartbeat of NOHT. We're proud to call this city home, and we're committed to investing in its creative community for the long haul.
